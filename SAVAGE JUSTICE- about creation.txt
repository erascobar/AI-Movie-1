CREATION PROCESS
I used flow for creation of the video, I start by figuring out the colors and camera scenes for the story, each "movie" or whatever it is has its own distinctive tones and directors cut. After I created the references for the girl who in my mind looked something like Elliot Page in the game Beyond Life, thus with few references I created something similar not to violate the policies of course.
With the reference I continued making different scene pictures and sceneries to get something that would fit the narrative I had in my mind, after that I would turn the referenced images into animation using Flow. I do combine Claude and ChatGPT to help me when I'm indecisive about certain aspects as for example I wasn't sure which color tones to use in last sequence of the movie "cliffhanger". 
For the voice all of the narration I created using elevenlabs. 
All of the content I edited in DaVinci Resolve.
